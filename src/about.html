About

Bio

Ray Austin is a professional music educator, pianist, and composer based in Portland, OR. Ray has been teaching music for over five years and has taught everyone from preschoolers to senior citizens. Ray has taught in summer camps, afterschool programs, non-profit organizations, community centers, and now in their private home studio. Ray also performs in various rock and folk bands in the Portland area.


Philosophy

Before opening my piano studio and teaching music full-time, I spent years working in childcare. I’ve been an afterschool program director, preschool teacher, behavioral therapist, and classroom aide, to name just a few. From these experiences I’ve learned that the key to a child’s heart and mind is through play. Learning should be fun! Learning to play the piano is no different. I teach through games, stories, imaginative exploration, and humor. Experiencing the joy of music doesn’t have to wait until you have years of study under your belt. In Sunshine Ray Piano Studio, making music is fun starting on day one!
