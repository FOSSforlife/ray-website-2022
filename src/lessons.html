Lessons

Overview & Rates

I teach 30 minute weekly piano lessons to students of all ages out of my tiny home located right off Woodstock in southeast Portland. Tuition for lessons is $120 paid monthly.


Teaching Approach

As a teacher, my primary goal is to instill within my students a love for creating music. My students learn how to read music, play with proper technique, feel rhythm, and understand music theory. These are the foundational skills, but my instruction goes much deeper. My students learn how to compose, arrange, improvise, play by ear, and jam with others in various styles. I believe that cultivating creativity and finding one's own artistic voice at the piano is just as important as developing technical proficiency. In Sunshine Ray Piano studio we do both, and much more!

What Do We Play?

Lessons in Sunshine Ray Piano Studio are different. In addition to being a formally trained pianist I am a singer-songwriter and gigging musician. I'm deeply tapped into the music young people want to listen to and create. Pop, rock, jazz, folk, film/videogame scores, classical- in my studio we do it all! I aim to give my students the tools to play whatever will inspire them and keep them excited about music for a lifetime!
